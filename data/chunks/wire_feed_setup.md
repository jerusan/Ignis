# Wire Feed Setup and Drive Roll Adjustment

## Settings Configuration

### Wire Diameter and Material Thickness Setup

1. Turn Left Knob to set wire diameter.

2. Turn Right Knob to set material thickness.

### Control Panel Navigation

1. Press Home Button on Control Panel.

2. Turn Main Control Knob until desired process appears on LCD display screen.

3. Press Main Control Knob to select process.

**Note:** Press Main Control Knob to go to next screen. Press Back Button to return to the previous screen.

### Process Settings Adjustment

4. Adjust settings for the selected process.

#### a. Polarity and Gas Settings:
- Plug cables in according to screen.
- Connect gas according to screen.
- Set SCFH between 20-30.

#### b. Auto Weld Settings:
- Turn Left Knob to adjust Wire Feed Speed (Amperage).
- Turn Right Knob to adjust Voltage.

**Note:** If the Wire Feed Speed or Voltage settings are adjusted manually, the white mark on the line shows the recommended setting for your wire/electrode diameter and workpiece thickness.

## Optional Settings

### Accessing Optional Settings

1. Press Main Control Knob to enter Optional Settings.

2. Turn Main Control Knob until desired setting appears on screen.

3. Press Main Control Knob to select desired setting.

4. Turn Main Control Knob to adjust setting.

### Available Optional Settings:

- **Run-In WFS** – Adjust wire speed before it contacts workpiece. This setting represents a % of the pre-set wire feed speed (WFS).

- **Inductance** – Adjust length of arc. Increase for more fluid puddle and flatter bead. Decrease for colder puddle.

- **Spot Timer** – Set timer for spot welding.

- **Recall Setting** – Retrieve saved settings.

- **Save Setting** – Program up to 5 different configurations. Turn Main Control Knob to scroll between 5 available slots. Press Main Control Knob to access OVERWRITE MEMORY SETTING screen and turn Main Control Knob to choose YES selection. Press Main Control Knob to save current Welder settings.

## Gas Shielded, Solid-core Wire Only

> **DANGER! TO PREVENT DEATH FROM ASPHYXIATION:**
> 
> **DO NOT open gas without proper ventilation. Fix gas leaks immediately.**
> 
> Shielding gas can displace air and cause rapid loss of consciousness and death.
> 
> **Shielding gas without carbon dioxide can be even more hazardous because asphyxiation can start without feeling shortness of breath.**

### Gas Setup Procedure

1. Open gas cylinder valve all the way.

2. Set Flow Gauge to SCFH value indicated on Settings Chart.

## Important Notes

**Note:** The initial settings may need to be adjusted after stopping and carefully inspecting the weld. Proper welding takes experience.
